# Xero

Go to **[login.xero.com/identity/user/login](https://login.xero.com/identity/user/login)** and log in.

Go to **[Your Company Name]** then **Settings**:

![](/xero-1.png)

Go to **Online payments**:

![](/xero-2.png)

Go to **Add another online payment option**:

![](/xero-3.png)

Choose **Custom Payment URL** then **Continue**:

![](/xero-4.png)

**Set:**
 - **Account name** to any name you recognise
 - **Your custom URL** to your Xero-compatible flow URL starting with `https://sticky.to/go/flow/`
 - **Pay now button text** to **Pay by card** or **Pay by bank** as appropriate

Choose **Save**:

![](/xero-5.png)

**Set:**
 - **Account name** to any name you recognise
 - **Your custom URL** to your Xero-compatible flow URL starting with `https://sticky.to/go/flow/`
 - **Pay now button text** to **Pay by card** or **Pay by bank** as appropriate

Go to **[Your Company Name]** then **Settings**:

![](/xero-6.png)

Go to **Invoice settings**:

![](/xero-7.png)

Go to **Options** then **Edit**:

![](/xero-8.png)

Set **Payment Services (Credit Card)** to the **Account name** you set earlier then choose **Save**:

![](/xero-9.png)

Your customers can now pay their invoices easily:

![](/xero-10.png)
