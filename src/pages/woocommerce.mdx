# WooCommerce

## Compatibility
Woocommerce 6.x and up to Wordpress 6.x.

## Requirements

PHP-BCMaths

## Download

https://cdn.sticky.to/plugins/WooCommerce-NoApplePay.zip

## Installation

1. Go to "Plugins"

2. Click "Add New" on the left

3. Click "Upload Plugin" at the top

4. Click "Choose file" and choose the downloaded zip file

5. Click "Install Now"

6. Click "Activate Plugin"

## Configuration

Gateway URL: https://pay.sticky.host
